How to get your business funded

Create a Funding Plan

Set up a business projectthat you want to finance.
We will finance 70% of the project cost and a 30% down payment
from you will have to be saved up from your business revenues.
Set an amount you commit to saving monthly to reach the 30% down payment.

Get Funded

On hitting the 30% savings threshold, you automatically get a loan for
the remaining 70% at single digit interest rates. You keep paying the same
monthly amount budgeted until the loan is fully repaid.

Cash flow management

To help you better manage your expenses and keep you on track
to reaching your goals, we offer sub accounts to improve your budgeting
and low interest overdrafts to meet emergency expenses

Mini cooperative

As a cooperative banking platform, our services are community
based to help keep you accountable. You can start or join a company of three or more
likeminded business owners focused on growing their businesses.

Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to get a loan?

That depends primarily on how long it takes to save up your 30% down payment. Once that threshold has been reached, we process disbursement of funds within 10 working days.

Can I get a loan when I bring the 30% down payment without saving it with you first?

You can only get a loan when you first save up 30% of the money you need on our platform as that helps us know if your business cash flow can sustain repayment.

How long is the repayment duration on your loans?

The loan tenor depends on how long it takes a business to save the 30% down payment and would fall between two to three times the savings duration. This is calculated so you can keep paying the same amount budgeted when saving to repay the loan.

Can I get a loan if I haven’t started or joined a mini cooperative on the app?

Only members of mini cooperatives can get a loan. Mini cooperatives are an essential part of how we can sustainably offer low interest, long term loans to businesses
